Max Temp

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 degrees latitude). It is very interesting to notice that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the title of the earth at the time of year this data was gathered.
